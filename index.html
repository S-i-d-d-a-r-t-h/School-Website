School Achievements

Weekly Test
We have 3-4 test every week

Every week, we have tests. We have ELA, spelling and grammar tests. We sometimes have math tests. Every 2 weeks, we get our progress reports. These show our scores, behavior, attendance, classwork and homework completion, and participation.
